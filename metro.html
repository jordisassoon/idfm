The Paris Metro Project

Metro Statistics

The Paris metro network is complex. The visualizations here breaks down some high-level statistics on lines and stations: busyness, size, development, and more.

To refresh your metro line memory, the map below shows a top-down view of Paris. Pick a metro line and see how the graphs change!

Timeline of Paris Metro Lines

Metro lines have evolved over the years. This interactive graph shows the year of establishment, and last year the line was extended. Double-click on a bubble to see the transition.

For a idea of line length, the bubble size depends on the number of stations it contains (hover over the bubble). You can also see the total length of the line in kilometers.

Bubble Chart of Station Busyness

Stations vary highly in their importance. The bubble chart tries to create a ranking of most to least important stations, based on the yearly traffic. The color of each bubble depends on the arrondissement the station is in. Hover over th bubbles!

Pie Chart of Line Traffic

Lines also vary highly in their importance. The pie chart tries to create a ranking of most to least important lines, based on the yearly traffic. Hover over th bubbles!
